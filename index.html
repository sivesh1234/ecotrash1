About

Since the 1970s, trashing has been a great tradition to celebrate the end of exams for Oxford students. However, this can have a dangerous impact on animal life and the environment.

EcoTrash sources eco-friendly trashing equipment and delivers it to you! In a composite bag, you will receive our trash pack; biodegradable confetti, coloured powder (made from 99% corn starch), party hats and shaving foam. We also include white sashes designed to have personalised messages for your friends.

With the Celebrations shop on Turl Street shut down, EcoTrash is the cheapest and most convenient option for students
