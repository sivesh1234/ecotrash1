About

Since the 1970s, trashing has been a great tradition to celebrate the end of exams for Oxford University students. However, this can have a dangerous impact on animal life and the environment.

EcoTrash is the new source of eco-friendly trashing equipment delivered to you! We sell trashing packages with biodegradable confetti, party hats, shaving foam and comes in a composite bag to reduce our impact. We also include white sashes designed to have personalised messages for your friends.

We believe you can both celebrate exams and care for the environment!
